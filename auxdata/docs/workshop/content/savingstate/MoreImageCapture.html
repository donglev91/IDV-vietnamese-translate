In this section, we will explore more image capture options to attain publication quality graphics
  1. Bring up two-panel display, [ht::menu New {View Window} {Map View} {Two Panes}]
  2. [ht::loadBasicGrid]
  3. In the display, select the first panel. You will notice a blue outline surrounding the panel.
  4. [ht::createBasicDisplay -display {Color-Shaded Plan View} -param {temperature}]
  5. In the display, select the second panel. You will notice a blue outline surrounding the panel.
  6. [ht::createBasicDisplay -display {Color-Shaded Plan View} -param {relative humidity}]
  7. For both panels, [ht::menu View Show {Display Scales}]
  8. Choose colors well suited for publication. [ht::menu Edit Preferences View] tab. Set background white and the foreground to black.
  9. Capture the image via [ht::menu View Capture {Image...}].
  10. Select "All Views" image capture

Your image is ready for publication in Science magazine.

After this exercise, switch back the foreground and background color to white and black respectively.
